About

Junior at Carnegie Mellon studying Mechanical Engineering and Robotics. I'm drawn to problems where mechanical design and software intersect: autonomous robots, assistive exoskeletons, precision mechatronic systems.

Past internships at Caterpillar (fuel system mechatronics) and RST Automation; this summer joining Symbotic's mechatronics team. Research on robotic exoskeletons at CMU's Robotics Institute and on autonomous UGVs for electrical substation inspection.
